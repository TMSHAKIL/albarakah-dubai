Best Bangladeshi Restaurant in Deira, Dubai

Al Barakah has been serving authentic Bangladeshi and South Asian cuisine in Deira, Dubai since 2015. Located on Al Rigga Road just minutes from Al Rigga Metro Station, we are one of Dubai's most accessible and beloved halal restaurants for the South Asian community and food lovers across the UAE.

Our menu is built around the rich culinary heritage of Bangladesh — from the royal Mughal-inspired Chicken Rezala to the street food favourite Beef Tehari. Every dish is prepared fresh daily by our head chef, who trained in Dhaka's finest kitchens for over a decade.

Biryani Restaurant in Deira Dubai

Our Kacchi Biryani is the dish Dubai talks about. Slow-cooked for over four hours with aged Basmati rice, premium halal mutton, and imported Bangladeshi spices, it is consistently rated among the best biryani in Deira by food bloggers and regular guests alike.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Al Barakah Restaurant in Dubai?

We are located on Al Rigga Road, Deira, Dubai — a 5-minute walk from Al Rigga Metro Station (Green Line). Parking is available on the side street.

Is Al Barakah Restaurant halal?

Yes, 100%. All our meat is sourced from Dubai Municipality certified halal suppliers. Our halal certificate is displayed at the entrance and available on request.

What is the best dish at Al Barakah Dubai?

Our most ordered dishes are the Kacchi Biryani, Chicken Rezala, and Beef Bhuna. The Kacchi Biryani is made fresh every day and sells out — we recommend ordering early or reserving.

Does Al Barakah deliver in Dubai?

Yes. We deliver to Deira, Bur Dubai, Al Qusais, Al Nahda, Mirdif and Al Muhaisnah. Order by phone or through Zomato and Talabat. Average delivery time is 35 minutes.
