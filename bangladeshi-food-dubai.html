Authentic Bangladeshi Food in Dubai

Dubai is home to one of the largest Bangladeshi communities outside Bangladesh. With hundreds of thousands of Bangladeshi workers, families, and professionals living in the UAE, the demand for authentic Bangladeshi food in Dubai has grown enormously.

Yet finding truly authentic Bangladeshi food in Dubai — not watered-down Indian food, not generic South Asian curry — is surprisingly difficult. Al Barakah was founded in 2015 for exactly this reason: to give Dubai's Bangladeshi community a taste of home that actually tastes like home.

Bangladeshi Community Hub in Deira

Deira has long been the heart of Dubai's Bangladeshi community. Al Barakah on Al Rigga Road sits at the centre of this community and has served as a gathering place for Bangladeshi families, workers, and visitors since 2015.

Bangladeshi Food for Eid in Dubai

Every year, hundreds of Bangladeshi families in Dubai celebrate Eid at Al Barakah. We offer special Eid menus, large family booking options, and our authentic Kacchi Biryani — the traditional centrepiece of any Bangladeshi Eid celebration.

FAQ

Bangladeshi Food Questions

Where can I find authentic Bangladeshi food in Dubai?

Al Barakah Restaurant on Al Rigga Road, Deira is one of Dubai's most authentic Bangladeshi restaurants. We import spices from Dhaka monthly and our chef trained in Bangladesh for 12 years.

Is Hilsa fish available in Dubai?

Yes. Al Barakah is one of the very few restaurants in Dubai that serves Hilsa fish — Bangladesh's national fish. Check with us for availability as it is seasonal.

Does Al Barakah serve food for Eid celebrations?

Yes. We offer special Eid menus and accept large family bookings for Eid celebrations. Our Kacchi Biryani is the centrepiece of every Eid meal at Al Barakah.

How far is Al Barakah from the Bangladeshi community areas in Deira?

Al Barakah is located on Al Rigga Road in the heart of Deira — 5 minutes from Al Rigga Metro Station and close to the Gold Souk, Naif, and Al Murar areas where much of Dubai's Bangladeshi community lives and works.
